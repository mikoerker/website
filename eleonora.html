Measuring Rotation and Reflectance Spectrum of Asteroid 354 Eleonora

Associated with Srednja Å¡ola Slovenska Bistrica

In this research project, I explored the properties of asteroid 354 Eleonora by measuring its rotation period and reflectance spectrum using advanced astronomical techniques. The project involved observing the asteroid through a telescope in Chile, capturing images in red, green, and blue filters, and analyzing the data to determine its rotational characteristics and spectral properties. Using photometry and spectroscopy, I calculated the asteroid's rotation period and compared its reflectance spectrum with data from the Gaia satellite. The results showed deviations of less than 20%, demonstrating the accuracy of the measurements. This project enhanced my skills in astronomical data analysis, photometry, spectroscopy, and the use of advanced telescope equipment.

Skills: Astronomy, Astronomical Data Analysis, Photometry, Data Processing, Scientific Reports, Statistical Data Analysis
